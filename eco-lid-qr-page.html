🌱 Eco Lid

What is this lid made of?

It’s made from heat-treated potato starch 🥔 and spent coffee grounds ☕ — both biodegradable materials sourced from food waste.

How to dispose of it?

☕ Used ➡️ 🌍 Compost ➡️ 🌱 Soil ➡️ 🪴 New Life

Compost it or bury it in soil — it breaks down naturally within weeks.

Why it matters?

Every biodegradable lid replaces approx. 3g of petroleum plastic and helps cut carbon emissions by up to 7g CO₂-eq.

Designed with purpose

This product is part of a sustainability-driven engineering project — showing that everyday waste can become meaningful design.
