🌍 Eco Lid

A Lid That Gives Back to the Earth

This lid is made from heat-treated potato starch 🥔 and spent coffee grounds ☕. It’s 100% biodegradable, zero-waste, and ready to help nature regenerate — starting with your cup of coffee.

3 Steps to Make a Difference

1️⃣ Enjoy your coffee – and keep the lid
2️⃣ Bury it in your garden or plant pot 🪴
3️⃣ Watch life grow – you helped a new plant take root 🌱

You don’t need to recycle – just return it to nature.

Designed With Purpose

This product is part of a final-year Mechanical Engineering project at the University of Birmingham. Its goal is to replace single-use plastic with something beautiful, compostable, and meaningful — all using food waste.
