A Lid That Grows

Compostable. Biodegradable. Meaningful.

From Sip to Seedling

Witness your coffee lid’s transformation from waste to wonder.

3 Simple Steps to Give Back

1. Sip: Enjoy your coffee as usual.
2. Plant: Place the lid in soil — garden or pot.
3. Grow: Watch it decompose and grow new life.

Why This Matters

Every year, billions of plastic lids contribute to pollution. Our Eco Lid helps reduce waste by returning nutrients to the earth — made from food waste, infused with seeds, and ready to grow. Your small act can make a big environmental difference.

Designed With Purpose

The Eco Lid is made of spent coffee grounds and paper pulp — both biodegradable and breathable. Its ergonomic shape fits standard cups and encourages eco-conscious behavior. A QR code links users to this very page — to remind them of the power of planting change.
