🌱 Eco Lid

Every morning, millions of plastic lids are tossed away.
But what if your coffee lid could do more than cover a cup?

A Lid That Grows

This lid is crafted from heat-treated potato starch 🥔 and spent coffee grounds ☕ — turning food waste into functional design. It's fully compostable and ready to return to the earth.

3 Simple Steps to Give Back

1️⃣ Enjoy your coffee – and keep the lid
2️⃣ Bury it in your garden or a plant pot 🪴
3️⃣ Watch nature take over – your lid becomes new life 🌿

Your small action completes a big cycle.

From Sip to Seedling

Witness how your Eco Lid returns to nature, nurturing new life.

Why This Matters

🌍 0% plastic
🪱 100% compostable
💨 Saves carbon with every use
🌱 Grows green where it lands
🌬️ Adds a breath back to Earth*

*Based on DEFRA (2023): ~7g CO₂ avoided per lid ≈ one human breath.
